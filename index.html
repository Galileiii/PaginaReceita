Coffee cupcake with whipped cream

The perfect cake to go with your daily coffee. It’s very easy and on top of that it’s delicious, it’s sure to impress. Make it and then tell me what you think!

Time: 1h10
Yield: 12 servings
Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cup (tea) of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of oil
  • 2 cups (tea) of sugar
  • 2/3 cup (tea) of cocoa powder
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 cups (tea) of ready whipped cream
  • Coffee beans for decoration
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions

In a blender, blend the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar, and cocoa powder until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, add the flour and baking powder, and mix with a spoon. Pour the batter into cupcake liners placed in a large baking tray, side by side. Bake in a preheated medium oven (180°C / 350°F) for about 25 minutes or until baked through and golden. Remove from the oven and let cool.
Para o recheio, leve todos os ingredientes ao fogo médio, mexendo bem até engrossar. Desligue, deixe esfriar e coloque dentro de um saco de confeiteiro com bico perle. Reserve. Coloque o chantilly dentro de um saco de confeiteiro com bico pitanga e reserve.
To assemble, make a hole in the center of each cupcake and fill it with the reserved coffee brigadeiro. Decorate the tops of the cupcakes with the reserved whipped cream, piping it in circular motions. Garnish with coffee beans and refrigerate until ready to serve.
